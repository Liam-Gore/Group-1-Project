Current Projects

Holographic Meals

We are looking to revolutionize the way we eat food in our society with our breakthroughs in holographic technology. We are coming close to discovering how to make holograms edible, which will make farming animals obsolete, along with grains, fruit, and even water. Our long term goal is to end world hunger, along with global warming though the widespread use of this technology.

Wormhole Travel

We have made incredible progress in our wormhole technology over the past 10 years, as we are now able to reliably send an apple through one of our generated wormholes and it will travel to somewhere on earth. Our research has shifted now to finding where all of our apples have ended up, as well as how to generate the wormhole without destroying any more planets.

FTL Drives

Our FTL Drives have been used in our time machines, but we are always upgrading and researching ways to make our designs more efficient and even faster. With the world being introduced to FTL Drives only within the last few years, we have found that there is much more to learn about them. We know this because we went to the future and saw that their FTL Drives were much smaller and faster than ours, so you know we have a long way to go!
